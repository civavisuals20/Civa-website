Questions you might have
What types of projects do you take on? +
VSLs - ads - reels - YouTube - SaaS explainers and more
I can handle pretty much everything. Just book a call and we'll discuss your project together.
How much does a video cost? +
The final price is defined during our first call, based on the type of video, length, amount of animation and your specific needs.
How long does delivery take? +
1 to 3 days for a simple edit. Up to 2 to 3 weeks for complex productions. A precise deadline is always confirmed before we start.
What languages do you work in? +
I speak French and English fluently, so communication will never be an issue.
What if I'm not satisfied? Are revisions included? +
Every project includes 2 revision rounds. After that, extra changes may be charged.
